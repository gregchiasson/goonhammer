<h2>Where to Read More</h2>
Thanks to the efforts of some of Black Library's best authors, the Night Lords have a few excellent books on hand for you to read. Here are some suggestions:
<ul>
 	<li><i>The Night Lords Trilogy (Soul Hunter, Blood Reaver, </i>and<i> Void Stalker) </i>by Aaron Dembski Bowden. The classic go-to text for Night Lords and post heresy Chaos Space Marines. This trilogy is considered a must read for any 40k fan and it's a fantastic look into the day-to-day lives of marines in the Traitor Legions following the Horus Heresy. <em>Void Stalker</em> also has one of the sickest fight scenes written in any 40k book.</li>
 	</ul>
